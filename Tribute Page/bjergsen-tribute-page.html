Here's a timeline of Bjergsen's professional career:

  • 1996 - born in Denmark.
  • April 2012 - Bjergsen joins Western Wolves.
  • June 2012 - joins Team-LDLC.
  • October 2012 - joins Copenhagen Wolves.
  • February 23, 2013 - makes professional debut as midlaner for Copenhagen Wolves.
  • March 1, 2013 - first competitive pentakill.
  • November 2, 2013 - leaves Ninjas in Pyjamas (formerly Copenhagen Wolves) to becom TeamSoloMid's starting midlaner for the 2014 NA LCS season.
  • December 15, 2013 - makes his TSM debut at the Battle of the Atlantic tournament.
  • April 2014 - wins first NA LCS Regular Season MVP.
  • September 1, 2014 - wins first NA LCS Championship with TSM. Awarded first Finals MVP.
  • March 16, 2015 - Wins IEM Season 9 World Championship, the only win in international play for an NA team.
  • March 2015 - Awarded second Regular Season MVP.
  • April 2015 - awarded second Finals MVP.
  • December 13, 2015 - wins All-Start 2015 1v1 Tournament.
  • July 2016 - awarded third Regular Season MVP.
  • August 2016 - wins NA LCS Summer 2016 Championship.
  • April 2017 - wins NA LCS Spring 2017 Championship.
  • August 2017 - awarded fourth Regular Season MVP.
  • September 2017 - wins NA LCS Summer 2017 Championship.
  • June 2018 - elected Vice President of the NA LCS Players. Association
  • Octber 14, 2019 - Bjergsen becomes part-owner of TSM.
  • September 2020 - wins NA LCS Summer 2020 Championship.
  • October 24, 2020 - Bjergsen retires as a player, moves to head coach position.
